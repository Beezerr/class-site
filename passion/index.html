Moths r Cool!

Drawn Like A Moth to A Flame

Moths are like the art of the bug world. With many different species and variations in wing size, shape and pattern it's easy to get swept away by their whimsical charm.

Here's some cool moth facts:

  • Moths outnumber butterflies by a 9:1 ratio
  • Male moths have a strong sense of smell
  • Most moths dont have mouths, so they can't eat
  • There are over 160,000 moth species
  • Some moths make squeaking noises to confuse predators
  • The Cercropia moth is North America's largest insect
  • Moths love beer and bananas
