KOROMARU FIGURE

This Koromaru figure is a tiny scale replica of the dog that comes along with a Shinjiro figure from the video game Persona 3. Koromaru or Koro for short is a shadow fighting dog that joins a team of high schoolers in taking down shadows in response to strange events in a tower called Tartarus that appears during the dark hours. He is part of a group called S.E.E.S which stands for Shadow Extracurricular Execution Squad, this abbreviation is shown on his arm band and on the armbands of the other members. Koromaru is the strength arcana and his persona is Cerberus which specializes in fire elemental attacks. Bestest Persona 3 character of all time ever!
